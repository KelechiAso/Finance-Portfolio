The Importance of Soft Skills

I'm learning about **soft skills**, which are personal attributes and non-technical abilities that enable effective and harmonious interaction with others. They are crucial for career success and professional development, complementing technical skills.

The Role of Soft Skills in Professional Success

While technical or "hard" skills are essential for performing a job, soft skills are the personal attributes that determine how well you work with others and adapt to various professional environments. They are the interpersonal and communication abilities that make you a valuable team member and a leader. For me, living in Nigeria, I've observed that a strong command of soft skills is often the differentiator that sets successful professionals apart, regardless of their technical expertise.
