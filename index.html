About Me

I am a passionate and results-driven financial analyst with a strong foundation in corporate finance, valuation, and data analytics. My work focuses on leveraging financial modeling and data-driven insights to help businesses make informed strategic decisions. I am highly proficient in Python for data analysis, and I use Excel and various visualization tools to build robust, dynamic models. My goal is is to apply my analytical skills to real-world business challenges and contribute to impactful financial strategies.

This portfolio is a collection of my personal projects, showcasing my abilities in financial statement analysis, discounted cash flow (DCF) valuation, and other analytical tasks.
